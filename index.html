Our Mission

While brainstorming ideas about our final project for Girls Who Code, chose an idea that we were passionate about, current events. Because our initial conversation included topics about presidential candidates, environmental issues, and human rights, we started to realize how uneducated we felt about current events. The internet allows us to have this information at our fingertips, but the amount of information becomes incredibly overwhelming for youth who are trying to become knowledgeable about current events. From this, we determined that the difficulty in finding simplified information causes many people to give up on following the news entirely. The goal for our final product is to have a website that centralizes all of the necessary information by having interactive infographics, quizzes, and links to unbiased sources.
